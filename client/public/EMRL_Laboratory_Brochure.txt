EMINENT MINES RESOURCES LIMITED (EMRL)
MINERALS STANDARD LABORATORY - CORPORATE BROCHURE

OVERVIEW
Eminent Mines Resources Limited (EMRL) operates a state-of-the-art Minerals Standard Laboratory in Nigeria, dedicated to providing high-precision analytical services for the mining, exploration, and environmental sectors. Our facility is equipped with cutting-edge instrumentation and staffed by seasoned geochemists and laboratory technicians.

CORE ANALYTICAL SERVICES
1. Mineral Identification & Characterization
   - X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) for elemental analysis
   - X-Ray Diffraction (XRD) for mineralogical composition
   - Specific Gravity and Bulk Density measurements
   - Particle Size Distribution (PSD) analysis

2. Precious & Base Metal Assaying
   - Gold Fire Assay (Pb Collection) with AAS or Gravimetric finish
   - Base metal analysis (Lead, Zinc, Copper, Iron, Manganese)
   - Strategic mineral testing (Lithium, Tantalum, Niobium, Tin)

3. Geochemical Exploration Support
   - Soil and stream sediment analysis
   - Rock chip and core sample preparation
   - Trace element analysis (ICP-OES/MS ready protocols)

4. Water & Environmental Engineering
   - Potability testing for mining camps and communities
   - Heavy metal contamination monitoring
   - pH, Conductivity, and TDS measurements
   - Industrial effluent analysis

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS & QUALITY ASSURANCE
- ISO 17025 compliant laboratory management systems
- Rigorous Internal Quality Control (IQC) using Certified Reference Materials (CRMs)
- Double-blind duplicate testing for 10% of all sample batches
- Participation in international proficiency testing programs

SAMPLE SUBMISSION GUIDELINES
- Samples should be clearly labeled and accompanied by a Sample Submission Form (SSF).
- Turnaround time (TAT): Standard (5-7 working days), Express (48-72 hours).
- Secure digital reporting via encrypted PDF and physical certificates.
